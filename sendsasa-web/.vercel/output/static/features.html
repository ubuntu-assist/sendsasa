Six Ways to Manage Money All From WhatsApp

Send Money

Remittance in 60 seconds

Send money to any mobile money wallet across Africa. Works with MTN, Orange, M-Pesa, Wave and more. Arrives in under 60 seconds, 24 hours a day.

TrustLock

Escrow for online deals

Lock your payment safely until you receive what you paid for. No more scams, no more "send first" arguments. Money only moves when both sides agree.

NjangiBot

Rotating savings circles

Run your tontine / njangi digitally. Members contribute each round and the full pot goes to one person. Completely automated, no cash chasing needed.

SplitChat

Group collections

Collecting money for a birthday, a trip, or a shared expense? SplitChat tracks who paid and who hasn't, and releases the money once you hit your target.

PayDay

Bulk payroll

Pay your whole team in one WhatsApp message. Say "pay Jean 15000, Marie 20000" and our AI does the rest. Up to 100 recipients in a single go.

SafiPay

Professional invoicing

Create a professional invoice in WhatsApp and share a payment link with your client. Get paid to your mobile money the same day. No accounting software needed.

A bank inside WhatsApp.

Send money home in 60 seconds — to any mobile money wallet across Africa. No app, no bank account needed.
