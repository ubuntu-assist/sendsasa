## PRODUCTS & SERVICES

SendSasa turns a WhatsApp conversation into a full-featured financial operating system for the African diaspora and their families on the continent. **No app download. No crypto wallet. No bank account required on either side. Just chat.**

### What senders can do

**Quote & lock FX instantly** — type into WhatsApp to see a live Stellar‑USDC rate in under 1 second; a firm SEP-38 quote locks the rate until payment is submitted.

**Fund in one tap** — approve an ACH bank pull (USA), Interac e-Transfer (Canada), or SEPA transfer (Europe); Circle mints USDC on Stellar and the transfer moves in seconds.

**Secure a marketplace deal** — lock USDC in a Soroban smart contract via TrustLock; funds release to the seller only when the buyer confirms delivery in chat. Disputes are adjudicated by Gemini AI with an on-chain verdict — no human arbiter, no escrow company.

**Pay staff from one WhatsApp message** — type "pay Jean 15,000, Marie 20,000, Paul 12,000"; Gemini AI parses it into a payroll draft; approve and a single Stellar batch transaction (100 ops max) pays everyone simultaneously. One transaction hash covers the entire payroll run.

**Run a cross-border savings circle** — create or join a NjangiBot tontine; diaspora members contribute in USD/EUR via Circle SEP-24; Cameroon members pay in XAF via MoMo; the pot payout is delivered in each recipient's local currency automatically.

**Invoice a European client in EUR** — SafiPay sends a SEPA payment link via WhatsApp; the buyer completes a standard bank transfer; Tempo anchor converts EUR → EURT on Stellar → USDC via Stellar DEX → XAF to the merchant's MoMo wallet in minutes.

**Collect from a group** — create a SplitChat pot with a short code; members join and pay from any rail; the organizer receives a single consolidated payout when the target is met.

**Smart contacts & recurring transfers** — save beneficiaries and pre-fill amounts for faster repeat payments ("Send 50,000 XAF to Mamá").

**Real-time receipts in chat** — every Stellar transaction hash and delivery status appears in the same WhatsApp thread; no separate app, no email.

---

### What receivers can do

**Receive money with no app, no bank account, no crypto** — XAF lands directly in an MTN MoMo or Orange Money wallet; the recipient needs only a SIM card.

**Get instant WhatsApp confirmation** — a "Funds delivered" message arrives the moment Onafriq's SEP-31 webhook fires; no polling, no waiting at an agent.

**Confirm escrow delivery in one tap** — a TrustLock seller receives a WhatsApp button to confirm service delivery; one tap triggers the Soroban contract to release USDC to their wallet.

**Participate in a savings circle** — contribute via Circle SEP-24 each cycle; receive the rotating pot payout in XAF via Onafriq SEP-31 when selected — the blockchain layer is invisible.

**Accept group collection invites** — join a SplitChat pot with a short code and contribute via Circle SEP-24; no account creation.

**View personal payment history** — incoming amounts, senders, purposes, and on-chain transaction IDs accessible directly in WhatsApp.

### What makes SendSasa different

**Chat-first, not app-first** — the entire 60-second flow lives in WhatsApp, which 97% of Cameroonian mobile users already trust and use daily. No competitor — not WorldRemit, not Sendwave, not Wave — operates natively in WhatsApp with Meta's encrypted in-chat Forms.

**Stellar-native settlement stack** — SendSasa uses Circle SEP-24, Onafriq SEP-31, Tempo SEP-24 (SEPA/EURT), MoneyGram Ramps SEP-24, SEP-38 firm quotes, Soroban smart contracts, and Stellar DEX path payments — not as isolated integrations but as a unified settlement backbone. No other Africa-corridor product combines all of these.

**Six financial primitives in one chat thread** — most remittance apps stop at "send money." SendSasa adds escrow (TrustLock), rotating savings (NjangiBot), group collections (SplitChat), bulk payroll (PayDay), and B2B invoicing with EUR/SEPA on-ramp (SafiPay). Every primitive settles on the same Stellar → Onafriq → MoMo rail.

**Cross-border tontine — a novel financial primitive** — no other platform lets a family member in Douala and their sibling in Paris participate in the same savings circle, each contributing and receiving in their own local currency. The XAF + USDC dual-pool composite payout is an original design not found anywhere else.

**Soroban smart escrow with AI arbitration** — TrustLock disputes are adjudicated by Gemini AI (`RELEASE | REFUND | MANUAL_REVIEW`) and the verdict is executed on-chain automatically. No human review cost on clear-cut cases; no centralized fund custodian.

**The first production XAF Stellar anchor** — there is no production XAF Stellar anchor today; the directory covers KES, TZS, NGN but not the CEMAC zone. SendSasa is building it, which opens 12 countries and 500M+ mobile money wallets to any Stellar wallet globally.

## BUSINESS MODEL & REVENUE STREAMS

### How do you capture value?

**1% platform fee on every transaction** (minimum 100 XAF / ~$0.17, maximum 2,000 XAF / ~$3.50)

This fee is deducted from the transfer amount before disbursement. Compared to the 6–10% industry standard for Africa-corridor transfers, SendSasa's 1% fee is highly competitive while remaining sustainable at volume. The near-zero Stellar base fees (~$0.00002/op) mean the platform's cost per transaction is sub-cent, making 1% highly margin-positive.

### Revenue streams by product

| Product | Fee structure | Unit economics |
|---|---|---|
| Send Money (remittance) | 1% of transfer | $0.70 avg on $70 avg transfer |

## SOROBAN CONTRACT — TECHNICAL DETAIL

The TrustLock escrow contract is built in Rust with `soroban-sdk` and compiled against Protocol 26 (Yardstick, CAP-82 checked arithmetic — the current Stellar mainnet protocol as of May 6, 2026). USDC is transferred via the SAC (Stellar Asset Contract) for Circle's native USDC (`GA5ZSEJYB37JRC5AVCIA5MOP4RHTM335X2KGX3IHOJAPP5RE34K4KZVN`) — no custom token wrapper. Authorization uses `require_auth()` per Soroban's invocation-time auth model.

```rust
pub fn lock(env: Env, client: Address, provider: Address, amount: i128, token: Address)
// client.require_auth()
// Transfers USDC from client to contract via SAC token::Client
// Emits: deal_locked(client, provider, amount, lock_time)

pub fn release(env: Env, client: Address)
// client.require_auth()
// Transfers USDC from contract to provider (minus 1% platform fee to fee_recipient)
// Emits: deal_released(client, provider, net_amount)

pub fn refund(env: Env, client: Address)
// Platform key only — called after Gemini AI verdict = REFUND
// Returns full USDC to client
// Emits: deal_refunded(client, amount)

pub fn auto_release(env: Env)
// Permissionless — callable by anyone after 72 hours
// env.ledger().timestamp() > lock_time + 259200
// Protects provider if client disappears
// Emits: deal_auto_released(provider, amount)
```

Contract events (up to 4 filterable topics) are emitted via `contract_event()` and ingested by the Horizon indexer's `getEvents()` call (7-day on-chain retention; archived to MongoDB for long-term audit storage). The NestJS backend invokes the contract via `@stellar/stellar-sdk/contract`'s `Client.from({ contractId, rpcUrl, networkPassphrase, signTransaction: platformKeySigner })`.
